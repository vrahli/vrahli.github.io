Student Projects


Here's a non-exhaustive list of projects I would be interested in supervising.

Formalization of programming languages

I'm interested in projects related to formalizing programming languages. For example, As part of our formalization of Nuprl in Coq, we have formalized Nuprl's programming language in Coq. I would, among other things, be interested in extending this language with new features, or to formalize other kinds of programming languages.

Formalization of logical theories

I'm interested in projects related to formalizing of logical theories. For example, As part of our formalization of Nuprl in Coq, we have formalized Nuprl's type theory in Coq; and as part of our formalization of KeYmaera in Coq, we have formalized its core in Coq. I would, among other things, be interested in extending these theories with new features, or to formalize other kinds of logical systems.

Implementation of distributed systems

Fault-tolerant distributed systems such as PBFT are nowadays heavily used in blockchain infrastructures (see our Asphalion paper for pointers). There are many proof-of-concepts that have been discussed in the literature, but that have never been fully implemented, and that are typically lacking important features such as state transfer or group membership changes. I'm interested in exploring such systems and extending them with features such as the ones discussed above.

Verification of distributed systems

In addition to implementing these distributed system, I'm interested in verifying their correctness, for example using our Asphalion tool.

Management of distributed systems

In order to implement distributed systems, one typically needs to implement some message passing infrastructure. I'm interested in building modular, configurable, and lightweight message passing infrastructures that make it easy to deploy distributed systems.

Blockchain systems

Byzantine fault-tolerant systems are more and more used in blockchain systems. However, they typically need to be adapted to fit the needs of those systems. I'm interested in supervising projects that aim at designing such tailored solutions.
